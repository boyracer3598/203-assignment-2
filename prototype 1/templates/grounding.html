Grounding

Benefits of grounding

Grounding helps pull way from negative or unwanted feeling or emotions

1.putting your hands in water, focus on the temperature of the water
2.pick up or touch an object, focus on the object focus on the texture,weight, and shape
3.breathing, focus on how your breath feels, the temperature of the air, and the sound of your breath
4.Enjoy food or drink, savor every bite or sip, focus on the taste, texture, and smell
