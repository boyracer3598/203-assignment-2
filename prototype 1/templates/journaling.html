Journaling

Benefits of Journaling

Journaling is a useful self reflection tool

How to Journal

using journaling prompts helps break down the reflection

values check-in: did follow your values today
Learning: what did you learn today
insight: were any interaction that gave you insight positive or negative
Dreams and desires: what your dream and step you can take tomorrow to get closer to your dream
Barriers and solutions: that problems you faced and how did you overcome them
